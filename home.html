About Us

Manzil, a word we hear many times in our life, a word that signifies destination, a word that seems to be end of hardships, for us Manzil is a beginning to find something above, to reach a place where no one ever visited, to reach a height which is not touched by anyone, this all may seems to be superfluous but that is Manzil all about, we put our every effort to achieve it but it changes the moment you reach it. For us Manzil is about imagination. एक ऐसा झूठ, जो सच से भी हसींन लगे।
